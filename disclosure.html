Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: April 16, 2026

The short version: TaterTV may earn a commission if you click certain links and make a purchase. It costs you nothing extra, and it never affects what we show you or how we show it.

How affiliate links work

TaterTV participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means that when you click certain links on our site and make a purchase or sign up for a service, TaterTV LLC may receive a commission from the merchant — at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate commissions help support the ongoing development and operation of TaterTV as a free service.

What this does not affect

TaterTV is a neutral TV guide. Affiliate relationships have no influence over the schedule data, show listings, or any content displayed on the Service. We do not promote, feature, or prioritize content based on commercial relationships. Everything you see is sourced from TVMaze and presented without editorial bias.

We will never recommend a product or service we wouldn't stand behind regardless of whether an affiliate relationship exists.

Current affiliate programs

TaterTV may participate in affiliate programs including, but not limited to:

  • Amazon Associates — As an Amazon Associate, TaterTV LLC earns from qualifying purchases made through links to Amazon.com.
  • Streaming service affiliate programs — We may link to streaming platforms and earn a commission for new subscriptions.
  • VPN and software affiliate programs — We may link to VPN services or other software and earn a commission for qualifying sign-ups.

This list will be updated as new affiliate relationships are established.

FTC compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials, TaterTV discloses its affiliate relationships clearly and conspicuously. Affiliate links may be identified by a disclosure note near the link or by referring to this page.
