About TaterTV

TV guide, schedule & calendar

We don't have opinions about what you should watch. TaterTV is a neutral, complete view of everything new airing in English — across every major network and streaming service — presented without an agenda.

Open it once a day, see what's on, move on with your life.

What TaterTV is

TaterTV is a TV guide for the streaming era. Broadcast, cable, and streaming have fragmented into dozens of services, each with its own schedule and its own app. TaterTV consolidates all of it into one fast, straightforward interface.

The focus is on what's new — new episodes, series premieres, and first airings in English. No reruns, no deep catalog rabbit holes, no content we think you might enjoy based on your history. Just what's actually on, right now and this week, across the US, UK, Canada, and Australia.

What TaterTV is not

Most TV sites are built to keep you on them as long as possible. TaterTV is built to get you off it as fast as possible.

  • Not a recommendation engine. No algorithm deciding what you'd probably like. We show everything new, equally.
  • Not a tracking app. No watchlists, no ratings, no "mark as watched." Those tools are useful — they're just not what this is.
  • Not a social network. No reviews, no community, no profiles. Just the schedule.
  • Not opinionated. We don't surface staff picks or editorialize about what's worth your time. That's your call.
  • Not complicated. No account required. No onboarding. Open it and it works.
