1. What information do we collect?

Information you provide

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you express interest in our services, participate in activities on the Services, or contact us. We do not process sensitive personal information.

Application data

If you use our app, we may collect the following if you grant permission:

  • Push notifications. We may request to send push notifications about your account or app features. You can opt out in your device settings.

Information collected automatically

When you visit or use the Services, we automatically collect certain technical information — such as your IP address, browser type, device characteristics, operating system, language preferences, and usage data. This does not reveal your specific identity and is used primarily for security, analytics, and to keep the service running properly.

We also collect information through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How do we process your information?

We process your information to provide, improve, and administer our Services, communicate with you, for security and fraud prevention, and to comply with law. We may also process your information for other purposes only with your prior explicit consent.

We may process your information to save or protect an individual's vital interest, such as to prevent harm.

3. What legal bases do we rely on?

We only process your personal information when we have a valid legal reason to do so. Depending on your location, this may include:

  • Consent — you've given us permission for a specific purpose. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligations — compliance with applicable law.
  • Vital interests — to protect your safety or the safety of others.

If you are located in Canada, we may also process your information under specific exceptions permitted by Canadian privacy law, including fraud detection, business transactions, insurance claims, or publicly available information.

4. When do we share your information?

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • Business transfers. In connection with any merger, sale of assets, financing, or acquisition of our business.

5. Third-party websites

Our Services may link to third-party websites or applications not affiliated with us. We are not responsible for their content, privacy practices, or security. Any data you provide to third parties is not covered by this Privacy Notice — please review their policies directly.

6. Cookies and tracking technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (such as web beacons and pixels) to maintain security, prevent crashes, save preferences, and assist with basic site functions.

We also permit third parties and service providers to use tracking technologies on our Services for analytics and advertising. To the extent these are considered a "sale" or "sharing" under applicable US state laws, you can opt out by submitting a request as described in Section 11.

You can set your browser to remove or reject cookies, though this may affect certain features. You may also opt out of interest-based advertising.

7. How long do we keep your information?

We keep your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this notice, unless a longer period is required by law. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it. If deletion isn't immediately possible (e.g. data in backup archives), we isolate it from further processing until deletion is possible.

8. Do we collect information from minors?

We do not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 18. By using the Services, you represent that you are at least 18, or that you are the parent or guardian of a minor consenting to their use. If we learn we have collected data from someone under 18, we will deactivate the account and delete the data. Please contact us at info@tatertv.app if you believe this has occurred.

9. What are your privacy rights?

Depending on your location (including the EEA, UK, Switzerland, Canada, and certain US states), you may have the right to:

  • Request access to and a copy of your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Restrict or object to our processing of your information
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@tatertv.app or submit a data subject access request.

If you are in the EEA or UK and believe we are unlawfully processing your data, you have the right to complain to your Member State data protection authority or the UK data protection authority.

10. Do-Not-Track features

Most browsers include a Do-Not-Track (DNT) setting. No uniform technical standard for recognizing DNT signals currently exists, so we do not respond to them at this time. California law requires us to disclose this. If an industry standard is adopted in the future, we will update this notice accordingly.

11. US residents — specific privacy rights

If you reside in California, Colorado, Connecticut, Delaware, Florida, Indiana, Iowa, Kentucky, Maryland, Minnesota, Montana, Nebraska, New Hampshire, New Jersey, Oregon, Rhode Island, Tennessee, Texas, Utah, or Virginia, you may have additional rights including:

  • Right to know whether we are processing your personal data
  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccuracies
  • Right to request deletion
  • Right to obtain a copy of data you previously shared with us
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights
  • Right to opt out of targeted advertising, sale of personal data, or profiling

We have not sold or shared any personal information to third parties in the preceding 12 months and will not do so in the future.

To exercise your rights, email Info@tatertv.app or submit a data subject access request. If your request is denied, you may appeal by emailing info@tatertv.app. If the appeal is denied, you may contact your state attorney general.

California "Shine the Light" Law

California residents may request information once per year about any personal information disclosed to third parties for direct marketing purposes. Submit such requests in writing using the contact details in Section 13.

12. Do we make updates to this notice?

Yes. We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time to stay compliant with relevant laws. The updated date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes may be communicated by posting a notice or sending you a notification directly.

If you have questions or comments about this notice, please contact us:

  • Email: info@tatertv.app
  • Company: TaterTV LLC

14. Review, update, or delete your data

Based on applicable law, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To do so, please submit a data subject access request or email us at info@tatertv.app.
